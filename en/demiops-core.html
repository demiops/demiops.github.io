Frequently Asked Questions

What benefits does Demiops Core offer compared to a public cloud like AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure?

Demiops Core allows you to host your systems and applications locally, giving you more control and security. Additionally, you can interconnect offices without complex network installations and leverage a hybrid architecture that connects with public clouds like AWS, Google, and Azure as needed.

How secure is Demiops Core?

Our platform uses Zero Trust Networks, ensuring that all access is rigorously verified through identification and authentication. This protects your systems against external and internal threats, keeping your data secure.

What kind of hardware do I need to install Demiops Core?

None, Demiops provides the hardware for Demiops Core when you start any of the subscription plans. However, Demiops Core is designed to operate with minimal and low-cost hardware, which reduces our subscription costs. Additionally, our 24/7 support team can help you configure the system if you need technical assistance.

What happens with system updates? Will they affect my operations?

Demiops Core updates are performed automatically with zero downtime, ensuring there are no interruptions in your operations or impact on your clients.

What kind of applications are available in the Demiops Core application hub?

The Demiops Core application hub includes key tools for your business, such as ERP systems, CRM, web portals, points of sale, and many other options designed to modernize and optimize your operations.
