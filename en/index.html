Frequently Asked Questions

What solutions does Demiops offer for business modernization and optimization?

Demiops provides products and services designed to transform and optimize your business:

Demiops Core: A local private cloud that connects systems and applications with high availability and advanced security.
Demiops Data: The sovereign data asset that captures, purifies, and projects your company's reality in real-time.
Demiops Intelligence: An artificial intelligence platform that automates processes, analyzes data, and improves decision-making.
Business Modernization: Consulting to implement advanced technologies like ERP, online stores, and cloud migration.
Professional Services: Strategic IT management based on ITSM and ITIL v4 to ensure efficient operations and continuous improvement.

What type of businesses can benefit from Demiops solutions?

Demiops is designed to serve a wide variety of industries and business sizes, including:

Factories looking to control their production with ERP.
Retailers wanting to optimize inventory or expand with online stores.
Law firms, medical clinics, and accountants needing AI for data analysis and automation.
Companies wanting to outsource their IT operations or adopt cloud technologies without prior experience.

How does Demiops guarantee the security of its clients' systems and data?

Demiops implements advanced technologies like zero trust networks and security strategies based on ITIL v4. This ensures complete protection of data and systems, with controlled access and constant monitoring.

Is it possible to integrate Demiops products and services with each other?

Yes! Our products and services are designed to be complementary. For example, you can use Demiops Core as a base to host ERP systems or custom solutions developed during the business modernization process. Additionally, Demiops Intelligence can be integrated to analyze data or automate processes on any of the platforms.

What support does Demiops offer during and after implementation?

Demiops provides comprehensive support at all stages:

During implementation: Our technical team works with you to configure and customize each solution according to your needs.
After implementation: We offer 24/7 support, automatic updates, and continuous improvement strategies to ensure that solutions stay optimized and aligned with your business growth.
