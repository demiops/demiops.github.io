Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to migrate all my current data for it to work?

Not necessary. Thanks to the Universal Query Engine, Demiops Data connects to your current sources —from old databases to isolated spreadsheets— and unifies them virtually. You start seeing the "Absolute Truth" without the risks or costs of a massive migration.

What does "Time Travel" in my data really mean?

It is the ultimate auditing capability. The system allows you to reconstruct the exact state of your company at any point in the past. You can compare today's performance against any historical date with surgical precision to understand real trends.

Does Demiops Data run on my own servers or in the cloud?

Demiops Data is agnostic and sovereign. It deploys over Demiops Core, our Hybrid Cloud architecture. This means you decide where your data engine physically resides: either on your own local hardware for maximum security or in your private cloud for elastic scalability.

Is it difficult for my employees to operate?

Technical complexity has been eliminated. By integrating with Demiops Intelligence, the system becomes conversational. Your team doesn't need to be an expert in complex code; just ask the AI, in natural language, to prepare your charts, cross variables, or generate detailed reports.

How do you guarantee that my data is an asset and not an expense?

By centralizing and optimizing how data is queried on your own infrastructure, we eliminate redundancy and unnecessary processing. This transforms chaos into a governed structure that serves as the high-quality fuel needed for your future sovereign AI models.
