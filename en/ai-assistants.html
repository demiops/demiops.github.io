Frequently Asked Questions

What type of tasks can an assistant like this perform?

From answering FAQs to generating contracts, coordinating agendas, writing emails, or integrating with business systems.

Can it be trained with my own company's information?

Yes. It can connect to internal knowledge bases, documents, emails, or databases to personalize its responses.

Do I need to know programming to maintain it?

No. Many of these assistants can be configured through visual interfaces or simply by providing reference content.

How secure is it?

It operates with encryption protocols, role-based access control, and complete auditing of every interaction. Additionally, it respects the privacy of sensitive data.

Can it integrate with my existing tools?

Yes. It connects with systems like CRM, ERP, email, calendars, and proprietary APIs, automating tasks within your usual workflow.

Does it work with customers and also with employees?

Correct. It can serve as an external assistant (for users or clients) or as an internal copilot to speed up team tasks.
