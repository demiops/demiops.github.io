Frequently Asked Questions

What differentiates this technology from a conventional chatbot or assistant?

An assistant responds. An agent acts. It can research, execute tasks, delegate functions, and decide complex courses of action by itself.

How do you ensure it doesn't make erroneous decisions?

It operates under rules, objectives, and limits defined by the organization. Additionally, it can submit its decisions for human review if desired.

Can it work with other existing systems?

Yes. Agents can integrate with CRM, ERP, internal APIs, or external tools, acting as intelligent intermediaries.

Can what they do be controlled or supervised?

Of course. Every action taken is recorded, with the possibility for auditing, review, and feedback.

Does it need a lot of training to work?

No. They are configured with objectives, rules, and context. The system learns and improves from real operation.

What type of tasks can an agent automate?

From researching purchase alternatives, to analyzing system errors, generating reports, coordinating with other agents, or preparing strategic responses.
